Ambience folder
===============

Drop your looping ambient bed here (wind, distant battlefield rumble, birds,
etc.) and assign it to "Battlefield Ambience" on the AudioManager component.

The AudioManager starts the ambience loop when a match begins and stops it when
you return to the menu. Its level rides the Master × SFX volume buses.
